About This Car

This is a clean 1997 Mazda Miata (NA generation) in classic white. It's one of the most fun and iconic roadsters ever built — lightweight, nimble, and endlessly rewarding to drive. Whether you're looking for a weekend canyon carver, a weekend track toy, or a head-turning daily driver, this car delivers.

I've owned the car for about 5 years, kept it garaged throughout, and taken good care of it — the service record reflects that.
